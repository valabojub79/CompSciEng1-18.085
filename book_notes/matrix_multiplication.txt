Matrix Multiplication:

**Columns** *multiply* **rows**.

Four main ways to regard matrix multiplication A * B.
1. (*rows* of A) * (*columns* of B)
  - C[i, j] = A[i, :] * B[:, j]
2. A times (*columns* of B)
  - C[:, j] = A * B[:, j]
3. (*rows* of A) * B
  - C[i, :] = A[i, :] * B
4. (*columns* of A) * (*rows* of) B
  - for (k <- 1 to n) C += A[:, k] * B[k, :]
